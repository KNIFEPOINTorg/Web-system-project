Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our delivery service

What areas do you deliver to?

Currently, we deliver within all 10 sub-cities of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We're working to expand to other major Ethiopian cities soon.

How fast is same-day delivery?

Our LFLshop Fast Delivery typically delivers within 2-6 hours during business hours (9 AM - 8 PM). Orders placed after 6 PM will be delivered the next day.

Can I track my delivery?

Yes! With LFLshop Fast Delivery, you get real-time GPS tracking. You'll receive SMS updates and can contact the delivery person directly.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

Our delivery team will call you before arrival. If you're not available, we can reschedule or deliver to a trusted neighbor with your permission.

Are there any delivery fees?

Yes, delivery fees are 50 ETB for LFLshop Fast Delivery and 35 ETB for ZayRide Delivery. Free delivery may be available for orders above certain amounts during promotions.
